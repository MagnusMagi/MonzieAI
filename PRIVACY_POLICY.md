# Privacy Policy for MonzieAI

**Effective Date:** January 27, 2025

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to MonzieAI ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application MonzieAI (the "App").

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Data
We may collect personally identifiable information, such as:
- **Contact Information:** Email address (when you sign in via Google/Apple or contact support).
- **Account Information:** User ID, profile preferences.

### 2.2 User Content
- **Images:** Photos you upload to the App for AI generation or enhancement.
- **Generated Content:** Images generated by the App using AI features.
- **Microphone/Camera Data:** Only accessed with your explicit permission for capturing photos or voice prompts.

### 2.3 Usage Data (Non-Personal)
- Device information (model, OS version).
- Log data (app crashes, performance metrics via Sentry).
- App usage patterns (features used, session duration).

### 2.4 Transaction Data
- Purchase history for subscriptions (processed securely by RevenueCat and Apple App Store/Google Play Store). We do not store your raw credit card information.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information to:
- Provide and improve the App's features (AI image generation).
- Manage your account and subscription.
- Respond to customer support requests.
- Monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve user experience.
- Detect and prevent fraudulent transactions or security incidents.

## 4. AI Image Generation & Third Parties
- **AI Processing:** We use third-party AI providers (e.g., Fal AI) to process your images and prompts. Your images are temporarily transmitted to these services solely for the purpose of generation/enhancement and are not used to train their public models without your consent.
- **Cloud Storage:** Your generated images and favorites are stored securely in our cloud database (Supabase).

## 5. Data Retention
We retain your personal data and content only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. You can delete your account and associated data at any time within the App settings.

## 6. Children's Privacy
The App is not intended for children under the age of 4 (or 13 depending on jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 7. Security
We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. However, no electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 8. Your Rights
Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you.
- Request correction or deletion of your data.
- Withdraw consent for specific data processing.

## 9. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
